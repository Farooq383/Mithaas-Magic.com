MITHAAS MAGIC – Where Sweets Seak Emotions

From Our Kitchen to Your Celebrations

Traditional mithai and modern confections — fresh, flavorful, and made just for you.

“Every bite is a memory in the making.”

At Sugar Bliss, we believe sweets are more than just food — they’re part of every celebration, emotion, and memory. From classic Indian mithai to fusion treats, every sweet we make is crafted with love and tradition, just like grandma used to make.

OUR SPECIAL

KAJU KATLI

Smooth, diamond-shaped cashew treats that melt in your mouth. Crafted with premium quality cashews and just the right touch of sweetness. A royal favorite for all occasions.

DOUBLE KA MEETHA

Made from fried bread slices soaked in saffron milk and layered with ghee and dry fruits, Double Ka Meetha is a royal treat from the kitchens of Hyderabad — rich, flavorful, and nostalgic.

KUNAFA

Our Kunafa blends golden shredded pastry with a soft, creamy filling, all soaked in a lightly floral sugar syrup and sprinkled with pistachios. It's a crunchy, gooey dessert that offers a luxurious experience.

GULAB JAMUN

Golden-brown milk-solid balls fried to perfection and soaked in rose-kissed sugar syrup. Whether served warm or chilled, our Gulab Jamuns are a heartwarming indulgence that never goes out of style.

RASGULLA

Soft, white balls made from fresh cottage cheese, cooked and soaked in light sugar syrup. Our Rasgullas are airy, juicy, and full of charm — a refreshing sweet perfect for any time.

RASMALAI

Rasmalai is a Bengali delight with fluffy paneer discs soaked in thickened, saffron-flavored milk. Every bite is a burst of creaminess, lightly sweet and aromatic — a perfect cooling dessert.
